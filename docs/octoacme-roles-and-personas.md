# OctoAcme Roles and Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure product changes are usable, accessible, and aligned with customer needs.

### Responsibilities
- Translate product goals into user flows, wireframes, and interaction patterns
- Validate assumptions with lightweight user research and usability feedback
- Define UX acceptance criteria with Product Manager and Developers
- Partner with QA/Test Engineer on usability and accessibility checks

### Interaction Points
- **Product Manager:** aligns on problem framing, scope, and expected outcomes
- **Developers:** clarifies behavior and edge cases before and during implementation
- **Project Manager:** informs schedule impacts for design and validation activities

---

## QA/Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA/Test Engineers protect product quality by validating acceptance criteria, reducing regressions, and making release risk visible.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy for features and releases (manual + automated)
- Build and execute test plans for critical and high-risk flows
- Partner with Developers to improve testability and defect prevention
- Track defects, verify fixes, and report quality trends

### Interaction Points
- **Developers:** collaborates on test cases, automation, and bug triage
- **Project Manager:** flags test risks and readiness for milestones/releases
- **Product Manager:** confirms acceptance criteria and customer-impacting defects

---

## DevOps/SRE

### Role Summary
DevOps/SRE enables reliable delivery by owning CI/CD health, observability, and operational readiness.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain deployment pipelines, release automation, and environment health
- Define monitoring, alerting, and runbook expectations for new changes
- Guide rollback planning and incident response readiness
- Partner with teams on reliability and performance improvements

### Interaction Points
- **Developers:** supports build/deploy workflows and production diagnostics
- **QA/Test Engineer:** aligns on test environments and release quality gates
- **Project Manager:** communicates operational constraints and release windows

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts turn business needs into clear, testable requirements and decision-ready analysis.

### Responsibilities
- Elicit, document, and refine functional/non-functional requirements
- Map business processes and identify gaps, assumptions, and dependencies
- Support backlog refinement with acceptance criteria and traceability
- Validate delivered outcomes against expected business value

### Interaction Points
- **Product Manager:** aligns requirements to roadmap priorities and outcomes
- **Project Manager:** tracks requirement risks, scope changes, and dependencies
- **Developers and QA/Test Engineer:** clarifies requirements during build and validation

---

## Sponsor / Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Sponsors/Stakeholders provide strategic direction, funding support, and decision-making for major trade-offs or escalations.

### Responsibilities
- Align project goals to business priorities and expected outcomes
- Approve major scope, timeline, and investment decisions
- Remove organizational blockers beyond the delivery team's control
- Review milestone status and support go/no-go decisions

### Interaction Points
- **Project Manager:** receives status, risks, and escalation updates
- **Product Manager:** aligns on value delivery and priority trade-offs
- **Team leads (Engineering/QA/DevOps):** engages when risk or incident impact requires leadership action
